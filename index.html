About the Owner

Justin High is the owner and head instructor at Gracie 717 North in Halifax, PA. He grew up wrestling on farms, brawling in schoolyards, and eventually competing at a high level in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. With a background that includes MMA training, collegiate education in exercise physiology, and real-world experience working security at top-tier nightclubs across the country, Justin brings a gritty, disciplined, and authentic presence to every class he teaches.

Under the mentorship of Damione Puopolo at Gracie 717, Justin sharpened his craft and developed a relentless competitive edge. He's competed against athletes from around the globe, earning five IBJJF world titles and a reputation for consistent performance, technical precision, and unmatched intensity. In 2022, he also began competing in ultramarathons, placing in the top 10 in each of his first three races.

Today, Justin continues to train, compete, and teach with the same focus that’s carried him through adversity and triumph. As a brown belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, he leads from the front—challenging his students to build discipline, trust the grind, and earn everything through hard work and grit.

"Fundamentals first. Pressure always. Discipline above all."
